# MegaMart

## Summary
An end-to-end analytics solution to monitor business performance.

## Project Business Scenario
MegaMart is a simulated omnichannel retailer based in Singapore, offering a wide range of products including electronics, fashion, groceries, and home goods. The company operates through an e-commerce website, mobile app and 50 physical stores across the country. MegaMart runs frequent marketing campaigns (email, app push, SMS) with A/B testing to optimize conversion and retention. The business is focused on scaling its digital channels while ensuring personalized customer engagement and operational efficicency.

## Business Goals
- Increase average order value
- Improve campaign ROI by identifying effective offers and channels
- Reduce customer churn through predictive models
- Understand customer segments for targeted marketing
- Track revenue trends and operational KPis across channels

## Business Questions
- What is our churn rate and who is at risk?
- Which customer segments are most valuable?
- How effective are different marketing campaigns?
- What products drive the most profit across channels?
